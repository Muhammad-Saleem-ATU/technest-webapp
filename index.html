About TechNest

TechNest is a fictional electronics and gadgets retailer. The application was built entirely using front-end web technologies with no back-end server required.

The aim of the project was to demonstrate practical skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Object-Oriented Programming by building a realistic eCommerce experience from scratch. The site includes product browsing, filtering, a shopping basket with localStorage persistence, and a full checkout flow with client-side validation.

Technologies Used
  • HTML5 and CSS3
  • Bootstrap 5 for responsive layout
  • Swiper.js for product carousels
  • Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
  • localStorage API for basket persistence

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to create an account to shop?

No, you can browse and add items to your basket without an account. Your basket is saved in your browser so it will still be there if you refresh the page.

How do I add items to my basket?

Click on any product to view its details page, choose a quantity and click the Add to Basket button. You can then continue shopping or go straight to checkout.

Can I change the quantity in my basket?

Yes. On the basket page you will see a minus and plus button next to each item. You can also remove items entirely using the × button.

What payment methods do you accept?

TechNest accepts all major debit and credit cards. Payment details entered on the checkout page are not stored or processed as this is a demonstration project.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 working days. Express delivery (1 to 2 working days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Can I return an item?

Yes. TechNest accepts returns within 30 days of purchase provided the item is in its original condition and packaging. Please contact our support team to arrange a return.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026. This is a demonstration document for a student project.

1. Information We Collect

TechNest only stores basket data locally in your browser using localStorage. No personal data is collected, stored on a server or shared with third parties. Any information entered on the checkout form is processed entirely within your browser and is not transmitted anywhere.

2. Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or analytics. The only browser storage used is localStorage to save your basket contents between visits.

3. Third-Party Services

This site loads Bootstrap and Swiper.js from CDN providers. These providers may log basic request data such as your IP address as part of normal CDN operation. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

4. Your Rights

You can clear your basket data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage through your browser settings or developer tools.

5. Contact

For any privacy related queries please contact us at support@technest.ie.
